A little bit about me

"I personally see photography as a hobby that I use to escape the stress of everyday life. I use my camera to capture moments that I would otherwise forget, moments which may have no importance to others- but they’re mine so it makes no difference to me. There’s a certain sense of freedom connected to it, not being bound by rules and limitations which others have set for you."
